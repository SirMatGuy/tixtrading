How does the bot analyze news?

Our AI uses natural language processing (NLP) to evaluate the content of a press release, assigning it a score based on relevance, potential impact, and overall sentiment.

How quickly will I get alerts?

You’ll receive alerts as soon as the bot detects relevant news and a rise in stock price, often within seconds of the market movement.

How accurate are the AI scores?

Our AI has been trained on a large dataset of stock news, making it highly reliable in detecting news with potential to impact stock prices. However, we recommend using the scores alongside your own analysis.

Can I use both the Momentum and Continuity bots together?

Absolutely! Our Pro plan give you access to both bots, allowing you to track both short-term momentum and longer-term trends.
